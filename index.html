When are my dog and I the same age?

Have you ever wondered when you and your furry friend will be the same age? Our dog age calculator makes it easy to find out! Follow these simple steps:

  1. Enter your birth date in the "My age" field.
  2. Enter your dog's birth date in the "Dog's age" field.
  3. If needed, adjust the human-to-dog year ratio (default is 7).
  4. Click the "Calculate" button and see the magic happen!
